Cost of Living Calculator

The Retail Price Index and Consumer Price Index are measures to calculate the cost of living based on a basket of goods and services over a defined length of time. It is typically updated monthly. However, the issues with CPI or RPI is, I find, they take goods and services that are not always representative of an average consumer, always focussing on their own pre-conceived assumptions. I find that for instance, inflation calculator for groceries, is highly undervalued, and so my inspiration to create this site is to allow for consumers to better record and self track their own inflation cost and see how it compares with the government statistics, while allowing for changes in spending habits or cutbacks to better weather the economic climate.
